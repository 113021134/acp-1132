Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free worldwide shipping on all orders above 1500 TWD. For orders below this amount, a flat shipping fee of 100 TWD applies. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days within Taiwan and 10-14 business days internationally.

How can I return a product?

We provide a 14-day return policy from the date of delivery. Products must be unopened and in original packaging. Please contact us at +889 987-664-344 to initiate a return; shipping costs are your responsibility unless the item is defective.

Are your fragrances authentic?

Yes, all our fragrances are 100% authentic, sourced directly from authorized distributors to ensure you receive genuine luxury products from brands like Chanel and Dior.

Do you offer gift wrapping services?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping for an additional 50 TWD. You can select this option at checkout to add a special touch to your fragrance purchase.

Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

Yes, you can request changes or cancellations within 24 hours of placing your order by contacting us at +889 987-664-344.
